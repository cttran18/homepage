The Basics

    Form and Stance

    For the basic orthodox kickboxing stance, keep your feet shoulder-width apart with your left foot in front of your right. Face forward with your chin tucked, and keep your arms up with your elbows tucked in to your ribs.

    Punches

    There are six basic punches: jab, cross, right hook, left hook, right uppercut, and left uppercut.

    Kicks

    There are a lot of ways to kick, but there are some basic kicks: front kick, roundhouse kick, and side kicks.

    Equipment

    Boxing Gloves (10oz, 12oz, 14oz)
    Handwraps (Traditional or Gel)
